About Mafia Random Death Generator

Mafia Random Death Generator is a program developed by TurnOffTheTV for turnoffthetv.github.io.
Mafia Random Death Generator is a text generator that randomly selects three objects and a scene to fit them in.
It also gets your name from a text field above it.
This program will not ever store information on your computer or on our servers.
